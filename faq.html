I created my YouTube channel to help me stay accountable during study sessions and to support others who might find it hard to study alone. It’s a way to connect and build a community of learners.

Do you have any tips for staying focused while studying?

Avoid loud music or songs with lyrics, as they can distract you. Instead, try soft instrumental music, lo-fi beats, or even ambient sounds like birds chirping. Also, keep your phone as far away as possible unless you absolutely need it for studying.
